Caucasian Ovcharka

The Caucasus Mountains are home to many of the oldest living molosser landrace dogs,
such as the Azerbaijani Volkodav, Azerbaijani Shepherd Dog and Georgian Shepherd Dog.
During the 20th century the Soviet breeders selected some of these varieties among Caucasian
dogs and created the Caucasian Shepherd Dog breed. The modern show class Caucasian
Shepherd is a hybrid of established Caucasian types, fundamentally based on the Georgian
Shepherd Dog, which is the largest and most muscularly built dog with attractive long hair.
